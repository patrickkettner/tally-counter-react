new features:

Introducing dark mode

You can embrace the darkness with the toggle in the top-right corner!

Mute notifications

Are you using the hotkeys (more information below) and you hate the notifications poping when the extension is closed? Mute notifications with the toggle in the top-left corner!

Color icon badges

Since you can mute notifications now, it would be nice if you had some visual feedback when pressing the hotkey. Introducing badges, handy little things that appear under the extension icon when you press the hotkey!
